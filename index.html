About CypherTalk

CypherTalk is an independent podcast dedicated to cybersecurity, privacy, and the human side of staying safe in a digital world. We sit down with researchers, practitioners, and experts exploring a broad range of topics: operational security, zero-knowledge cryptography, artificial intelligence in both attacks and defences, threat intelligence, privacy technology, and the evolving regulatory and ethical landscape of data protection.

Our guests come from across the security spectrum — offensive researchers, privacy engineers, threat intelligence analysts, policy experts, and educators. What unites them is a commitment to making complex security topics accessible and actionable.

CypherTalk is educational content first. Whether you're a security professional, a developer, or someone who just wants to understand the threats shaping our digital lives — there's something here for you.
