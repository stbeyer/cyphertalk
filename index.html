About CypherTalk

CypherTalk is a podcast produced by Oak Security, one of the leading blockchain security audit firms. We sit down with the researchers, founders, and practitioners who are building a more secure decentralised ecosystem.

Topics include smart contract security, cryptography, zero-knowledge proofs, operational security, protocol design, and the evolving threat landscape in Web3.

Hosted by Stefan Beyer, Founder & Managing Director of Oak Security.
